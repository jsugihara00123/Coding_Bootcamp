Bio: A little about Coldplay

They are a British rock band that formed in London in 1996. The band only consists of 5 individuals listed below:

  • Main vocalistand pianist - Chris Martin
  • Guitarist - Johny Buckland
  • Bassist - Guy Berryman
  • Drummer - Will Champion
  • Creative Director - Phil Harvey

The band members original met at University College London. They started playing music together between 1996 and 1998. The bands first or original name was Pectoralz and then Starfish.
